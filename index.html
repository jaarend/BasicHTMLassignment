All About Clippy

Did you know Clippy's real name is Clippit?

That's right. It was only later on that it was called "Clippy".

In fact, the Microsoft Office Assistant had a number of helpful interactive characters like:

  • The Dot
  • Hoverbot
  • The Genius
  • Office Logo
  • Mother Nature
  • Scribble
  • Power Pup
  • Will (a caricature of William Shakespeare)
